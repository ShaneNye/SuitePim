Welcome, !

This Platform serves a Management Tool for Lot Numbered Inventory Items within the Sussex Beds Netsuite Enviroment for the Product Development & Website Teams.

Initial Functionality will consist of;

  • Viewing Exsisting Item record data within netsuite via a custom dashboard
  • Editing any given datapoint and pushing update to netsuite
  • Saving 'Work In progress' updates for future analysis or scheudling to update
  • Updating WooCommerce Pricing policy & web fields
  • Raising support cases directly to jira for issues or change requests
